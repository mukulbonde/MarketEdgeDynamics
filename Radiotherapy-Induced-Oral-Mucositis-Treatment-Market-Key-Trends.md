Recent studies indicate that approximately 80% of patients receiving radiation therapy for head and neck cancers develop oral mucositis. This painful condition can seriously affect patients' quality of life and complicate their cancer treatment. The oral mucositis treatment market is expected to reach 500 million dollars by 2025, with new therapies leading the way.

Laser therapy: Laser therapy Low-level LLT (LLLT) is is being explored for its potential to relieve pain and promote healing. Clinical trials are demonstrating its effectiveness in reducing the severity of mucositis.

Patients are becoming increasingly involved in their treatment decisions, leading manufacturers to focus on developing easy-to-use products. Social media platforms such as Reddit and Quora reflect growing communities of patients sharing experiences, underscoring the need for effective treatments.
