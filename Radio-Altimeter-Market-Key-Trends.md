Technological Advances
Recent innovations in radio Altimeter technology has significantly improved performance and reliability. The introduction of dual-frequency altimeters, using Ku-band and L-band frequencies, has improved accuracy in various weather conditions. This is crucial for modern aviation, where accurate altitude measurements are vital to safety during landings and takeoffs.

Regulatory bodies such as the FAA and The EASA are reviewing guidelines to incorporate these technological advances. The implementation of updated standards ensures that radio altimeters meet strict safety and performance requirements, particularly in environments affected by 5G networks.
